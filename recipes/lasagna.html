World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

The World's Best Lasagna recipe is a rich, hearty, and classic Italian-American dish made with layers of seasoned ground beef and sausage, a flavorful tomato-based sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Baked to perfection, this lasagna has a deliciously cheesy, meaty, and slightly tangy taste, making it a comforting favorite for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion & Garlic: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices & seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How to make the Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
